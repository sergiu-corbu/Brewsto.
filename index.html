About us

We are a team of 6 young friends who united their creativity and skills to create what is best for you and your tastebuds: a specialty coffee from the best importers from around the world. All of our flavours & syrups are made in-house with 100% natural ingredients (no chemicals or preservatives added), ensuring you the highest quality in taste & health. Sprinkled with our uniqueness, we invite you to test the best and be our guest, and find out what the fuss is about!
